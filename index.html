About the Journal

The Indian Journal of Commercial Law and Constitutional Governance (IJCLCG) is a peer reviewed academic journal focusing on the intersection between commercial law, market regulation, and constitutional governance.

Call for Papers

We invite submissions from scholars, practitioners and students on topics relating to commercial law, constitutional governance, regulatory law, corporate governance and economic regulation.
